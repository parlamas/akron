My school life started with kindergarten when I was five. I remember my elder sister walking me there in the morning and I hated it, but once I got there I had a good time. When I was seven, elementary school started. The teacher was a cruel lady who only liked the good students and, although I was one them, I resented her for talking down those whom she thought were bad ones. Other than that, I had a great time at elementary school. When I was thirteen, high school started. At those times, high school lasted six years. I remember savoring and really enjoying all six of them. Lots of everything: adventure, flirting, learning, discussing, partying...you name it. After that, it was OK but nothing comparedd to high school. I studied Classical Greek in Athens, Business in New York and Philosophy in Copenhagen. I cannot complain...I'd be most ungrateful if I did... ........................................... Mit skoleliv startede med børnehaven, da jeg var fem. Jeg kan huske, at min storesøster gik med mig der om morgenen, og jeg hadede det, men da jeg kom dertil, havde jeg det godt. Da jeg var syv, startede folkeskolen. Læreren var en grusom dame, som kun kunne lide de gode elever, og selvom jeg var en af dem, ærgrede jeg mig over, at hun talte ned om dem, som hun mente var dårlige. Bortset fra det havde jeg det rigtig godt i folkeskolen. Da jeg var tretten, startede gymnasiet. Dengang varede gymnasiet seks år. Jeg kan huske, at jeg virkelig nød dem alle seks. Masser af alt: eventyr, flirte, lære, diskutere, feste...you name it. Derefter var det OK, men intet sammenlignet med gymnasiet. Jeg studerede klassisk græsk i Athen, Business i New York og filosofi i København. Jeg kan ikke klage...jeg ville være meget utaknemmelig, hvis jeg gjorde det...
