Gorm and Carla invite guests (p. 6)

 

Gorm and Carla have just moved.1 They have bought a house out of town.2 Before, they used to live in a little apartment in the center.3 They were glad to live in the center and they were happy about their neighbors, but they were tired of the city's noise.4 They also needed more room and they wanted their own house.5
Now they live in a big house with a nice garden.6 It is located near a lake and a forest.7 Gorm and Carla are happy about the house and the enjoy having plenty of space.8 But they don't know anyone nearby and they miss their old neighbors.9 'Why don't we invite Mads and Line?', asked Carla.10 'Sure, we can definitely do that,' says Gorm.11 Carla goes in and calls and Line answers the phone.12
