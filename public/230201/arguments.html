You can look forever, but you will never find anything that in a strictly identical context and identical circumstances is both the right and the wrong option or choice. This is proof that there exist right and wrong.

There are many people who do not feel comfortable with the concepts of 'right' and 'wrong,' so they find it convenient to claim that there is neither right nor wrong. But in saying that, they are not aware that what they are saying in effect is that it is wrong to believe that there is right and wrong and it is right to believe that there is no right and wrong, which means that they believe that there is right and wrong even though they foolishly attempt to deny it.

Der er mange mennesker, der ikke føler sig trygge ved begreberne 'rigtigt' og 'forkert', så de finder det bekvemt at påstå, at der hverken er rigtigt eller forkert. Men når de siger det, er de ikke klar over, at det, de i virkeligheden siger, er, at det er forkert at tro, at der er rigtigt og forkert, og det er rigtigt at tro, at der ikke er rigtigt og forkert, hvilket betyder, at de tror, at der er rigtigt og forkert. er rigtigt og forkert, selvom de tåbeligt forsøger at benægte det.

Many have tried to reduce the complexity of life to a dipole. One dipole is progressive versus traditional or conservative; another is fair versus unfair; another is good versus bad; yet another, family versus the individual; change versus stability and so on and so forth.

But only one dipole can stand rigorous philosophical and logical scrutiny: right versus wrong.

Mange har forsøgt at reducere livets kompleksitet til en dipol. Den ene dipol er progressiv versus traditionel eller konservative; en anden er fair kontra uretfærdig; en anden er god mod dårlig; endnu en, familie versus individet; forandring versus stabilitet og så videre og så videre.

Men kun én dipol kan tåle streng filosofisk og logisk undersøgelse: rigtigt versus forkert.

Talerens eller forfatterens identitet er irrelevant. Det, der er relevant, er, hvad taleren siger og gør.
